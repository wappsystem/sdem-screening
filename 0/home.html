Emotional Memory and Sleep

Research Study



Welcome to the Emotional Memory and Sleep Research Study
We invite you to participate in an exciting new research study that involves testing how sleep may affect memory for emotional information in older adults with and without depression.

Eligibility
You may be eligible to participate in this study if you are:
• Aged 50 - 90 years
• Fluent in English


If eligible, you will need to come to the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research in Glebe, Sydney on two occasions, once for a short daytime screening visit and once for an overnight sleep study. During the overnight study, we will measure aspects of your sleep using novel sleep imaging technology and memory before and after sleep. You will receive reimbursement for your time.

Your participation in this study is voluntary.

If you are not eligible your data will not be used.
